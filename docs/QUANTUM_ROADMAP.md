# Quantum Roadmap

Orphan Radar is classical today.

Quantum algorithms are not implemented in this repository. The MVP makes no claim of quantum advantage and has no dependency on quantum hardware.

## Candidate future research questions

- Could a quantum-walk graph ranker improve routing on very large knowledge graphs?
- Could amplitude amplification help in very large, weakly structured candidate-edge search spaces?
- Could QAOA-style edge-set selection help optimize review queues under constraints?

## Non-goals

- No current quantum implementation.
- No hidden quantum stubs.
- No claim that quantum helps ordinary note vaults.
- No frontier-math branding in the running product.
