# Project Brief

## Problem

Knowledge workers capture more information than they connect. Disconnected notes create hidden waste: duplicated research, forgotten ideas, and weak institutional memory.

## Product hypothesis

A local-first graph engine can improve knowledge quality by identifying orphan notes and suggesting reviewable connections without silently changing the source corpus.

## Design constraints

- Local-first processing.
- No external APIs.
- No LLM dependency.
- No source mutation.
- Explainable candidate edges.
- Human review before promotion.
- Classical MVP; future quantum research is explicitly out of scope for the running core.

## Why it matters

As AI increases the volume of generated notes and research, knowledge systems need maintenance workflows that preserve trust.
